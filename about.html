About Dr. Arhin

Mr. Arhin is a passionate and dedicated lecturer with a deep-rooted expertise in computer networking and systems administration. With over a decade of experience in academia and industry, he has mentored countless students into becoming skilled IT professionals. He holds a Master’s degree in Information Technology and is certified in several industry-standard networking protocols and systems, including Cisco (CCNA), CompTIA Network+, and Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA). Mr. Arhin combines theory with real-world practice, making his classes both engaging and impactful.
